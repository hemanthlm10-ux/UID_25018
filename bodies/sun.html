THE SUN
G-type Main Sequence Star
// Overview

The Sun is the star at the heart of our Solar System — a colossal sphere of hot plasma that accounts for 99.86% of all the mass in the Solar System. It generates energy through nuclear fusion in its core, converting hydrogen into helium and releasing the light and heat that makes life on Earth possible. At 1.39 million kilometres across, around 1.3 million Earths could fit inside it.
